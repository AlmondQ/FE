Flex

容器属性

flex-direction

row,row-reverse,column, column-reverse

flex-wrap

nowrap, wrap, wrap-reverse

flex-flow

是flex-direction和flex-wrap的简写形式，默认为row nowrap

justify-content

flex-start,flex-end,center,space-between,space-around

align-items

flex-start,flex-end,center,baseline,stretch

align-content

应用在多个轴线之间，单个轴线不起作用flex-start,flex-end,center,space-between,space-around,stretch

项目属性

order

order:<integer>项目排列顺序，数值越小，排列越靠前，默认为0
1
2 order:-1

flex-grow

flex-grow:<number>定义项目的放大比例，默认为0，即不放大
1
2

flex-shrink

flex-shrink:<number>定义项目的缩小比例，默认为1
1
2 shrink:0，不缩小
